By His Excellency Richard Earl of Bellomont Captain General and Governor in
Chief of His Majesty's Province of New York, etc. A PROCLAMATION
WHEREAS His Majesty by his Letters Patents has appointed me Captain General
and Governor in Chief of the Province of New York. etc. I have therefore
Thought fit by the Advice of his Majesty's Council to Continue all Officers
Civil and Military within the said Province, in their respective Offices and
places until further order, and all his Majesty's Subjects are hereby required
to comport themselves accordingly.

Given at New York, the 2 d Day of April in the Tenth Year of his Majesty's
Reign Annoque Domini School. Bellomont.

God Save the KING.
Printed by William Bradford, Printer to the Kings Most Excellent Majesty in
the City of New York, School.
