At a COUNCIL held at Boston September. 8. 1670.
THE Council taking into their serious Consideration the low estate of the
Churches of God throughout the World, and the increase of Sin and Evil amongst
our (...) following us for the same, Do therefore (...) and twentieth of this
instant (...) Public Humiliation throughout this Jurisdiction, and do commend
the same to the several Churches, Elders, Ministers and People, solemnly to
keep it accordingly: Hereby prohibiting all Servile work on that day.

By the Council, Edward Rawson Secret.
