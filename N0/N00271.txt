AT A General Court Held at BOSTON May. 16. 1683.
THis Court Considering that Cattle are Rated to the Country at a greater
value than they will yield from Man to Man:

Does therefore Order, that henceforth all Neat Cattle shall be Rated to the
Country, viz. Oxen at four years old and above, at three pounds a piece; all
Cows, and Steers at three years and above, at forty shillings a piece; and all
of two years old, at thirty shillings a piece; and Yearlings, at fifteen
shillings a piece; and Swine at a year old, at ten shillings.

By the COURT Edward Rawson Secr't.
