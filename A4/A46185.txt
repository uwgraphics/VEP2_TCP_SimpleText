DIEV ET MON DROIT

HONI SOIT QVI MAL Y PENSE

royal blazon or coat of arms
By the Lord Deputy.
OSSORY,

WHEREAS the Parliament stands Prorogued until the Seventeenth day of January
next; And whereas for sundry Causes and Considerations; We have thought it fit
and convenient and necessary further to Prorogue the said Parliament until the
twentieth day of February next; We do therefore will and require all Persons to
whom it does or shall appertain to take due notice thereof, to the end that in
the mean time they may repair to their Houses or otherwise apply themselves
about their private Affairs at their pleasures, And then to give their
attendance here again, at the day and time aforesaid, in the usual place
appointed for that intended Service, whereof they may not fail.

Given at the Council Chamber in Dublin the nineteenth day of December, 1664.
DVBLIN, Printed by John Crook, Printer to the Kings Most Excellent Majesty,
and are to be sold by Samuel Dancer in Castle-street, 1664.
