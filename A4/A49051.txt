November. 12. 1642.
YOU are to give notice in your Pulpit tomorrow, that all such of your Parish
as have Listed themselves to go for Soldiers, or any other that are willing
to go in this service, do repair to the New Artillery Ground by eight of the
clock on Monday morning, And to encourage them to go on with cherfulness.
And if any man have any old Saddles, and will bring them into Blackwell Hall
either on monday or tuesday next, for such as are Dragooneres, it shall be
well accepted.

ISAAC PENNINGTON, Major.
