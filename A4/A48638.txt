A LIST OF ALL THE ADVENTURERS IN THE Mine-Adventure.
May the First, 1700.
Note, Those marked *** are qualified by their Shares to be Elected of the
Select Committee. And those marked ** are qualified to Vote in a Grand
Committee; And those marked *, are qualified to Vote in a General Meeting: And
no Person has more then one Vote.

*** The most Noble his Grace the Duke of Leeds Governor.

*** Sir Humphrey Mackworth Deputy Governor.
