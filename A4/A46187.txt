DIEV ET MON DROIT

HONI SOIT QVI MAL Y PENSE

royal blazon or coat of arms
By the Lord Deputy.
OSSORY.

WHEREAS the Parliament stands Prorogued until the Tenth day of April next:
And whereas for sundry Causes and Considerations; We have thought it fit and
convenient and necessary further to Prorogue the said Parliament until the
second day of May next; We do therefore will and require all Persons to whom it
does or shall appertain to take due notice thereof, to the end that in the mean
time they may repair to their Houses or otherwise apply themselves about their
private Affairs at their pleasures, and then to give their attendance here
again, at the day and time aforesaid, in the usual place appointed for that
intended Service, whereof they may not fail.

Given at the Council Chamber in Dublin, the thirtieth day of March, 1665.
