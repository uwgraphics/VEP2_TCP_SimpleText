Apill 15. 1643.
YOu are required to commend to God in your Prayers, the Lord General, the
whole Army employed in the Parliaments Service, and the Design undertaken by
them. As also in your Sermons, effectually to stir up your people now to
appear in person, and to join with the Army, to stand up for our Religion and
Liberties, as is desired and expected by the said Army, and the Committee for
the Militia, in this City.

ISAAC PENNINGTON, Mayor.
