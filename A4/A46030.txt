GENERAL GOVERNOR of IRELAND.
ORMONDE,

FOR special Reasons conducing to His Majesty's Service, We do hereby Require
and Command all Officers of His Majesty's Army, now in this City, or any other
part of this Kingdom, absent from their Commands and Charges, immediately upon
Publication hereof, to repair to their several and respective Garrisons or
Quarters, and there to attend their duties, and not to absent themselves from
the same, without special License from Us, notwithstanding any License
heretofore given by Us.

Given at His Majesty's Castle of Dublin, the fifth day of January, 1666. G.
LANE.

GOD SAVE THE KING.
DVBLIN: Printed by John Crook, Printer to the Kings most Excellent Majesty,
and are to be sold by Samuel Dancer in Castle-street, 1666.
