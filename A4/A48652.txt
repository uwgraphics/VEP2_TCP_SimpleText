Chamberlain, to be waiting on their Majesty's the several Months in the Year.

DR. Horneck. Dr. Bright. Mr. Kidder. Dr. Fowler.

Dr. Tillotson. Dr. Stillingfleet. Dr. Stratford. Mr. Young.

August.
Dr. Belk. Dr. Hough. Mr. Wiggan. Mr. Williams.

Dr. Sharp. Dr. Craddocke. Dr. Edwards. Mr. Staino.

Dr. Goodman. Dr. Beveridge. Dr. Freeman. Dr. Turner.
