Tuesday May 8. 1660.
Resolved upon the Question by the Commons assembled in Parliament, England and
Wales, and the Town of Barwick upon Tweede, for which they serve, the
PROCLAMATION for Proclaiming of the Kings Majesty, to the intent the same may
be Proclaimed in the respective Counties, Cities, and Boroughs; Which the
several Sheriffs, Mayors, Bailiffs, and other Head Officers are required to do
with speed accordingly.

W. JESSOP Clerk of the Commons House of Parliament.

London, Printed by Edward Husbands and Thomas Newcomb, Printers to the Commons
House of Parliament.
