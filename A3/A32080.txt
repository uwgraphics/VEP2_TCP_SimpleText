BY THE KING.
A Proclamation to Declare, that the Procez of Green Wax may be sealed at
Oxford as well as at London, for the Courts of King's Bench, Common Pleas and
Exchequer, during these times of this unhappy distraction.
HIs MAjESTY having received information from several persons, and from
several parts of this His Kingdom, that the Legal proceedings in the great
Courts of Justice at Westminster, the Court of King's Bench, the Court of
Common Pleas, and Exchequer, are much hindered because the Seals for those
Courts commonly called the Green Wax Seals, are kept and continued in London
only, whether many of His Majesty's Subjects dwelling in the Western and
Northern parts, and other parts of this Kingdom, dare not resort for their
Procez to be sealed in these times of general distraction; But if His Majesty
should ordain that Seals should be kept at the City of Oxford, as well as at
London, for the sealing of the Procez, of those Courts whether all Suitors, and
their Atturneys, Clerks, and Sollicitors, might have a free and safe access
for the suing out and sealing of their necessary Procez returnable in those
