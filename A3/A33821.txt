Harpsichord, Theorbo, Lute, or Spinett.

Set to Music by Mr. Gillier.

LONDON, Printed by J. Heptinstall, for Henry Playford at his Shop in the
Temple-Change, Fleetstreet. School.

Where may be had the Proposals of the Musick-Lottery, as also Dr. John Blow's
New Harpsichord-Book.

To my Honored Friend, Capt. WORTLEY.
Honored Sir,
HAving your Opinion and Approbation of the Composing These my First SONGS in
English; and knowing of your being a Judge, do think my self Obliged to
Dedicate them to you, hoping the favor of your Acceptance, which will be an
Honor to,

Sir, Your most Humble Servant, GILLIER.
