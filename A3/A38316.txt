Die Iovis 22 Ianuarii 1645
THE LORDS and Commons in Parliament assembled do Order and appoint this day
fort-night for a day of Thanksgiving, for taking in of DARTMOUTH, to be kept
within the Cities of London and Westminster, Lines of Communication, and ten
miles about; And this day three weeks for all other places in the Country.

ORdered by the Commons assembled in Parliament, That this Order be forthwith
printed published and dispersed into the several Counties of the Kingdom.

Hen. Elsynge, Cler. Parl. Dom. Com.
