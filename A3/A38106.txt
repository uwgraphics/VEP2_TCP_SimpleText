Die Sabbathi, viz. 18. Junij. 1642.
Resolved upon the Question, by the Lords and Commons assembled in Parliament,
Leycester is against Law, and against the Liberties and Property of the Subject.

Die Lunae 20, Junij.
Resolved upon the Question, THat all those that are Actors, in the putting of
the Commission of ARRAY in execution, Shall be esteemed as Disturbers of the
Peace of the Kingdom, and Betrayers of the Liberty of the Subject.

Ordered by the Lords in Parliament assembled, That the abovesaid Votes shall
be forthwith Printed and Published.

Jo: Brown, Cleric: Parliamentorum
