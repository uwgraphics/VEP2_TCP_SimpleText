Thursday 11th of May, 1654 At the Counsel at White Hall.
ORdered by his Highness with the consent of his Counsel, That the
Commissioners for the Excise for the time being, and their Sub-Commissioners,
do demand and receive for the duty of Excise from and after the 25th of March
last, six pence upon every hundred weight containing one hundred and twelve
pound of Iron English, and no more, to be paid by the Maker; the rate in the
Ordinance of the 17th of March, 1653. notwithstanding.

Ex. Wm. Iessop Clerk of the Council.
