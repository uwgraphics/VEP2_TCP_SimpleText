The CASE concerning Monmouth Election.
AT the Election for a Burgess to serve in this present Parliament for the
Borough of Monmouth in the County of Monmouth, (which Borough sends but one
Burgess) there were Competitors,

The Honorable CHARLES Lord HERBERT, Eldest Son to the Marquess of Worcester;
And JOHN ARNOLD Esquire

Mr. Arnold had the Majority of Votes; but the Mayor of Monmouth unduly
Returned the Lord Herbert.

The Merits of this Election are to be heard before the Committee of Elections
and Privileges, on Wednesday the 24th day of this Instant November, 1680.
