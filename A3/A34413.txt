A CATALOGUE OF THE LIBRARY OF Mr. HENRY COOK, PAINTER, Deceased.

Which will be sold by Auction at his late Dwelling House in Bloomsbury-Square,
near King Street in Bloomsbury, on Monday the 30th instant, at Three afternoon.

By JOHN BVLLORD.

Catalogues to be had at Mr Parkers at the Unicorn under the Royal Exchange, Mr
Buck, near the Temple, Mr Barnes in the Pall Mall, Booksellers, and at the
Place of Sale.

Conditions of SALE.
1. THat he who bids most is the Buyer, and if any difference arise which the
Company cannot decide, the Book or Books to be re-exposed to Sale.

2. That all the Books in this Catalogue for ought we know are perfect, but if
any appear otherwise the Buyer to have his choice of taking or leaving them.

3. That the Money for the Book or Books so bought, be paid within six days
after the Sale is ended, at the place of Sale.

4. That no person advance less than 6 d. each bidding, after any Book arises
to the sum of 10 s

5. That the Books shall be exposed to View three days before the Sale begins.

Any Commissions may be sent to J. Bullord.

Sold by J. Nicholson, Tho. Newborough, and J. Bullord.
