Die Sabbati, 24. August. 1644.
IT is this day Ordered and Ordained by the Lords and Commons assembled in
Parliament, That the Sergeants and Counselors at Law, in the several Counties
of this Kingdom, within the power of Parliament, shall execute the Commissions
of Oyer and Terminer, and Jail-delivery, wherein they are nominated
Commissioners, notwithstanding any Statute or Clause therein to the contrary.

Hen. Elsynge, Cler. Parl. D. Com.

Die Sabbati, 24. August. 1644.
IT is this day Ordered and Ordained by the Lords and Commons assembled in
Parliament, That Commissions of Sewers shall be granted by the Commissioners of
the Great Seal, for the several Counties of this Kingdom, within the power of
the Parliament; although the Lord Treasurer, or Lord Chief Justices be absent.

Hen. Elsynge, Cler. Parl. D. Com.

London, Printed for Edward Husbands. 1644.
