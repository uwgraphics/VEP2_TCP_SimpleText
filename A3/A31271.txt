A CATALOGUE OF Excellent Books, in Greek, Latin and English; CONSISTING Of
Divinity, History, Philosophy, Geography, Poetry, and Miscellanies, in all
Volumes. Which will be sold by Auction at Toms Coffee-House adjoining to
Ludgate, on Monday the 15th of October, 1694; beginning at 3 in the Afternoon,
and so continue daily till all be sold. CONDITIONS of SALE. 1 HE that Bids most
is the Buyer, if any Difference arise, which the Company cannot decide, the
Book to be exposed again to Sale. 2 The Books for ought we know are Perfect, If
any appear otherwise before they are taken away, the Buyer may take or leave
them. 3 That every Person be obliged to give is his Name and Place of Abode,
paying also 5 s. in the Pound for what he buys if demanded; and be obliged to
take his Books away within (...) days after the Sale is ended.

Catalogues are Distributed Gratis at. Mr. Partridges at Charing Cross, Mr.
Metcalfes in Drury Lane, Mr. Hargraves in Holbourn, Mr. Joys under the Royal
Exchange, Booksellers. And at the Place of Sale.
