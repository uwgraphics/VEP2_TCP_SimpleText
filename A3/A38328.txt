Die Sabbathi 15o Junij 1689.
ORdered by the Lords Spiritual and Temporal in Parliament Assembled, That no
Papist, or reputed Papist, do presume to come into the Lobby, Painted Chamber,
Court of Requests, or Westminster Hall, during this Session of Parliament. And
it is further Ordered, That this Order be Printed and Published, and set upon
the Doors of the said Rooms.

JO. BROWN Cleric' Parliamentor'

LONDON, Printed by Charles Bill, and Thomas Newcomb, Printers to the King and
Queens Most Excellent Majesty's. 1689.
