The CASE concerning Westbury Election.
AT the Election for Burgesses to Serve in this present Parliament for the
Borough of Westbury in the County of Wilts, there were Competitors

WILL. TRENCHARD Esquire EDW. NORTON Esquire HEN. BERTIE Esquire RICH. JEWS Esquire
Mr. Trenchard and Mr. Norton had it by the Majority of Votes, but the Mayor of
the Borough unduly Returned Mr. Bertie and Mr. Jews.

The Merits of this Election is to be heard before the Committee of Elections
and Piviledges on Monday the 22th of this Instant November, 1680.
