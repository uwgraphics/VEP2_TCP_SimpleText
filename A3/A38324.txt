AN ORDER OF THE Lords Spiritual and Temporal Assembled at Westminster, For the
Not-observing the Sixth Day of February.
Die Sabbati, 2 Febr. 1688.
ORdered by the LORDS Spiritual and Temporal, Assembled at Westminster, That
the Day of Thanksgiving, on the Sixth of February Instant, on which day the
late King JAMES the Second came to the Throne, shall Not be observed in this
Kingdom.

Jo. Brown Cleric' Parliamentorum.

In the Savoy: Printed by Edward Jones, and for James Partridge, Matthew
Gyllyflower, and Samuel Heyrick, 1688.
