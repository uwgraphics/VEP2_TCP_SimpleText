To the Kings Most Excellent MAJESTY.
The Humble Petition of James Percy.
SHOWS,
THAT this is the 11th. Year of Your Petitioners Claim to the Title and Earldom
of Northumberland.

That the 9th. Instant at 10. of the Clock, is appointed the day of Hearing,
before the Right Honorable Lords Spiritual and Temporal in Parliament
Assembled.

Therefore he most humbly Prays, that Your Majesty would be graciously pleased
to be present at the hearing of the Cause, and then Your Majesty will
understand how the Moon has been set under the horrid Black Cloud of
Oppression, and to see the Moon rise into the Serene Horoscope of Justice.

And Your Petitioner shall Pray, etc. JAMES PERCY.
