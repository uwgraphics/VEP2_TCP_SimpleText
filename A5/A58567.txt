ACT For security of the Protestant Religion.
EDINBURGH, April 28. 1685.
OUR SOVEREIGN LORD, with Consent of His Estates in Parliament Conveened,
Ratifies and Confirms all the Acts and Statutes formerly past for the Security,
Liberty, and Freedom of the True Church of GOD, and the Protestant Religion,
presently professed within this Kingdom, in their whole Strength and Tenor, as
if they were here particularly set down and expressed.

EDINBVRGH, Printed by the Heir of Andrew Anderson, Printer to His most Sacred
Majesty: ANNO DOM. 1685.
