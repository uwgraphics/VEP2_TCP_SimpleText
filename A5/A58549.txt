Edinburgh, May 8. 1685.
OUr SOVEREIGN LORD and Estates of Parliament, Do hereby Declare, that the
giving, or taking of the National Covenant, as Explained in the Year 1638, or
of the League and Covenant, so commonly called; or VVriting in defense thereof,
or ovvning of them as lavvful or obligatory on themselves, or others, shall
infer the Crime and pains of Treason.

EDINBURGH, Printed by the Heir of Andrew Anderson, Printer to His most Sacred
Majesty, Anno Dom. 1685.
