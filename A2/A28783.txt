THe Book of Subscriptions, for Insuring of HOUSES (by the City of London) in
case of FIRE, is now Open, wherein several Aldermen and Others have Subscribed
for a considerable Number of HOUSES; and the same will so Continue in the
CHAMBER at the GVILD-HALL, where constant Attendance will be daily given from
Nine of the Clock to Twelve in the Forenoon, and from Three of the Clock to Six
in the Afternoon, for Entering the Subscriptions of all such Persons as shall be
willing to INSURE.

Printed by S. Roycroft, Printer to the Honorable City of London.
