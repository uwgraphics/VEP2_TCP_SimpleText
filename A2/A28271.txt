A BLACK LIST Of the NAMES, or Reputed NAMES, of Seven Hundred Fifty Two Lewd
and Scandalous Persons, who, by the Endeavors of a SOCIETY set up for the
promoting a Reformation of Manners in the City of London, and Suburbs thereof,
have been Legally Prosecuted and Convicted, as Keepers of Houses of Bawdry and
Disorder, or as Whores, Night-Walkers, etc. And who have thereupon been
Sentenced by the Magistrates as the Law directs, and have accordingly been
Punished (many of them diverse times) either by Carting, Whiping, Fining,
Imprisonment, or Suppressing their Licenses. All which (besides the Prosecution
of many Notorious Cursers, Swearers, Sabbath Breakers, and Drunkards, not here
incerted) has been effected by the Society aforesaid.
Published for the satisfaction of such as are Contributers toward the
necessary Charges of this Undertaking, and for the Encouraging others to give
further Assistance for the more effectual carrying on so great and so hopeful a
Design.

NOTE, B. signify Bawd, W. Whore, D. H. Disorderly House, D. P. Disorderly
Person, and P. P. Pick-Pocket.
