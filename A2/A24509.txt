An ACCOUNT OF MONEYS Disbursed out of the Tonnage and Poundage for the NAVY,
and for HIS MAJESTY'S Private Use.

AN Account of such Moneys as have been paid into the Receipt of His Majesty's
Exchequer, by the Collectors of the Customs sithence the 25. day of May, 1641.
Collected upon the several Bills of Tonnage and poundage, And since that time
disbursed for the use of the Navy and toward the support of his MAJESTY unto
the ninth of June 1642.

Majesty's receipt in the Exchequer, sithence the 25. of May, 1641. As by their
accounts appears the sum of Two hundred thirty eight thousand, two hundred
