IE prend, I Take, Ie ne prend pas: I take not: Prends-ie? Take I? Take I not?
Ne prends-ie pas? Thou take, Tu prends, Thou take not: Tu ne prends pas:
Take thou? Prends-tu? Take thou not? Ne prends tu pas? He takes, Il prend,
He takes not: Il ne prend pas: Takes he? Prend il? Takes he not? Ne prend il
pas?

VERBES IMPERSONELS.
COmme les Anglois vsent ces signs it, there, ou they, deuant leurs verbes
impersonels, ainsi nous auons, on, lon, ou il, deuant les nostres: comme AS
Englishmen use these signs it, there, or they, before their verbs impersonals,
so we have, on, lon, or il, before ours: as They, or men did drink. On boit,
ou l'on boit. They did drink. On beuuoit. They drank. On beut. They have
drunk. On a beu. They had drunk. On auoit beu. They shall drink. On, ou l'on
boira.
