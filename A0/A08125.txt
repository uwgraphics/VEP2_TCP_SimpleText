Though for the more convenient and plentiful accommodation of each planter it
were to be desired that they carried the provisions of victuals above said, if
their estates would reach thereunto, yet they may (having means to take fish
and foul) live comfortably that want all the rest, Meal for bread only
excepted, which is the staff of life.
