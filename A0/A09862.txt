A right strange and woonderful example of the handie work of a mighty God,
to move us wretched sinners to amendment of our wicked lives, by this
lamentable Spectacle for all men and women to behold, of the birth of three
children borne in the parish of PASKEWET, in the County of Monmouth, on
Thursday, the third of February last. 1585. And are are as this present to be
seen at London. ^awde
Ioane Io^^
