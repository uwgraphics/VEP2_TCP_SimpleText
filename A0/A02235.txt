B'ethel: OR A form for families, in which all sorts of both sexes, are so
squarde, and framde by the word, as they may best serve in their several
places, for useful pieces in God's buildinge. By Matthew Griffith, We are
laborers together with God. 1. Corinth's. 3. 9.
