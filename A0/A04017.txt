
depiction of St. RochThe graces following be granted to all the brethren and
systers benefactours and good doers unto the hospytall of ye blessed confessor
saint Rock founded and establyssyd wtin the of Cite Excester yt day that they
do say a pater noster an Aue / and a Creed it is granted them that they shall
never be infecte nor grieved with the stroke of ye pestylence as more plainly it
does appear in his legende how and when al mighty god granted this petycyon
to the said blessed confessor saint Rock / and sent yt by his angel Raphaell
