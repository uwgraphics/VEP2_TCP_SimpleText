ADVERTISMENT.
BEGIN your Psalter with devout kneeling, or at leastwise with bowing to
Jesus, and say thus as follows:

In the name of JESUS let every knee bow, of the celestialls, terrestrials,
and infernals: and every tongue confess, that our Lord Jesus Christ, is in
the glory of God the Father.

And at the end, either of the first Five, Ten, or last Fifteen, say:

Our Lord Jesus Christ humbled himself, being made obedient unto death: even
the death of the Cross.
