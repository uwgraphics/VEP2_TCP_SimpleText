diagram of human body as microcosm (with members corresponding to zodiacal
signs)

In head and face is Aries place,
and Taurus in the neck:
But arms and hands in Geminies bands,
do suffer joy and wrecke.
Cancer does possess the breast,
the ribs and stomach stout:
And Leo back and heart he has,
thereof ye need not doubt.
Virgo belly and bowels,
take thou with all their pains,
For Libra lo the buttockes has,
the navel and the raines.
Scorpio the private parts,
to have lo still she cries:
For sure she is that Sagittarie,
possesses both the thighs.
Then Capricorn must rule the knees,
for legs to Aquarie long.
And Pisces purchased has the feet:
therefore do him as wrong.
