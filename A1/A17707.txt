Gabriel Friend his Prognostication, for the year of our Lord Jesus Christ.
M. D. XCVI.

Wherein is contained an Astrological description of the four quarters of the
year: and also his judgment of the daily disposition of the weather, and
other matter meet and necessary for such a work.

Imprinted at London, by Richard Watkins and James Robertes.

Cum priuilegio Regiae Maistatis.
