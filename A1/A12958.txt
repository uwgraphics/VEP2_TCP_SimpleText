Steven Steple to mast Camel*
REst ye merry vayre zyr, I trow ye be maste Camel.
icha^ on message to you zent, vrom goodma^ Geffrai Chapel
A hate ye zent a bottell a hay, and bad command ma tie ye,
And vorde dyng he spoke ye to, a preyd ye dat ye wood hy ye,
And zuch kind daintrels as a had, zum draf he hate ye zent,
A zyerd ye vor to gnab deron, vor der is no better in Rent,
It Is good and zoote and alzo new. to mende your zyckysh brain
Vor we will a zeete ye ha great stud, and zet your self to pain,
Him vraid yer wits wud zore ye veil, ye gyn to rave zo zone,
A dynkte ye will be mad, al out, beuore dat May be done:
And much he merueilt dat ye wud, zo chorlshly to vin wryghte,
And dat ye zent vm such an Anser, dat zounded noding right
To Harry Hoball zyr ye wrote, as pearit in your letter,
zwap yer speckles up se nase, and look about ye better,
And Anser Geffray Chapel zyr, dat took ye de zupplicacion,
Vor his name is not Hary whobal, ich zwear by gods zauacion
How zay ye now yore speckles be on, can ye vorstande his byll,
ych ween a trete ye (zereuerence a you) to do Churchard no ill
And her cha brought yor byll again, corrupt it or ich go,
Vor vende gods vorbod man I zedge, to let it go vorth zo:
But well ich zee yor brain is dicke, yor wits be curstly vexed,
Prey God ye be not zyde yor zelf, er be to morrow next:
Deruore go couch and sleap a now, and dan com to yor part,
And dyte a wyser dyng dan dat, or all is not wort a vart.
Now ych ha mine arnde a do,
Chud ha ye yor head to heed a
And ve good master Churchard to,
And zo God be yor sprede a.
Imprinted by Richard Lant.
