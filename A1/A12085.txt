CERTAIN HELPS AND REMEDIES Under GOD to prevent Dearth and Scarcity, ALSO
To kill and destroy Heath, Brakes, Moss or any other Shrubs whatsoever, And to
Improove the said Ground.

With diverse other points of Husbandry never yet practiced by any.

If there be any that cannot perceive, not believe how barraine Land not worth
xij pence the Acre, may be Improved and be made to yield XX. or XXX. shillings
an Acre, let them but help the Author unto some such barraine Land, and they
shall soon see it effected, if they please.

LONDON, Printed by B. ALSOP. 1638.
