A Bait for Momus A SERMON, Preached long since in the Town of Bedford.
By TOBIAS BLAND.
1. TIM. 4. Verse. 1. 2. 1. Now the Spirit speaks plainly. That in the latter
times, some shall depart from the faith, and give heed unto spirits of error,
and doctrines of Devils.

2. Which speak lies through hypocrisy, having their consciences seared with
a hot iron, etc.

LONDON, Printed by John Raworth for Joseph Hunscott, and are to be sold at his
house in Crane-court, in the Old-change. 1639.
