DOVE. AN Almanac for the year since the nativitie of our LORD, 1635.

Being the third after bissextile or leap-yeare; and from our Saviors passion,
1602.

Being also the year from the Worlds creation, 5584.

Calculated properly for the famous Universitie and Town of CAMBRIDGE, where
the pole is elevated above the horizon 52 degrees and 17 minutes.

Inter spem, curamque, timores inter, and iras,
Omnem creed diem tibi diluxisse supremum.
Printed by the Printers to the University of Cambridge.
