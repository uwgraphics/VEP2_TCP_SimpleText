Sir,
YOu are entreated to give notice in public this next Lords day the 15 of
August, that Master Christianus Ravius, heretofore public Professor of the
Oriental Tongues in some Universities beyond the Seas, will begin a Lecture of
these Tongues in London-House, God willing, upon Thursday come seven-night the
26 of this instant August; and that he will preface to that Lecture on Thursday
next at three of the clock in the afternoon in the place aforesaid.
