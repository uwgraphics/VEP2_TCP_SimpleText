THE TRIAL OF TRUTH: OR, A discovery of false PROPHETS.

Containing a plain and short Discovery of the chiefest points of the Doctrine
of the great ANTICHRIST, and of his Adherents the false Teachers* and Heretics
of these last TIMES.

By E. P.
