SIR,
YOu are requested to make your appearance on Friday next at Scriveners-Hall,
between two and three of the clock in the afternoon, to advise with diverse
other persons of quality, which will be then and there present, how to secure
just debts upon honorable Propositions to the Parliament for advance of
moneys. Whereof you are not to fail, as you tender your own good.

This 14 August, 1644.
