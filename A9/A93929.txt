OXFORD One day Stage-Coach.
THese are to give Notice to all Persons that have occasion to go to Oxford by
Coach; Let them repair to the Greyhound in Holborn, where they may be furnished
with a good Coach and able Horses, which sets forth every Monday, Wednesday,
and Friday for Oxford, performing the Stage in one day; and sets forth from the
Mitre in Oxford for London, every Tuesday, Thursday, and Saturday; performed if
God permit, By

Widow STONEHILL. JOHN FOSSET.

The Stage begins Monday next, being the 17th instant April, and sets forth
precisely at Six in the Morning.
