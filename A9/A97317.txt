Die Sabbathi 19. Decembris, 1646.
IT is Ordered by the Lords in Parliament Assembled, that the City Printer
shall Print and publish the two Petitions presented to this House this day; the
one entitled, The humble Petition of the Lord Major, Aldermen, and Commons of
the City of London, in Common Council assembled; And the other entitled, An
humble representation of the pressing greivances, and important desires of the
Well-affected Freemen, and Covenant-engaged Citizens, of the City of London;
And the Answer of the Lords in Parliament made this day unto them both.

John Brown
Cler. Parliamentorum.
