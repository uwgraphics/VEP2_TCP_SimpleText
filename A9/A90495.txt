A Perfect Table of Three hundred forty and three Victories obtained since the
Kings attempt to enter into Hull at the beginning of these Wars, July 26. 1642.
to September. 14. 1646. by their Excellencies the Earl of Essex, and Sir Thomas
Fairfax, Captains Generals of the Parliaments Forces.
Robert Earl of Essex, his Excellence, General of ye Army, Employed for the
defense of the Protestant Religion, the safety of his Maties Person, and of the
Parliament, the preservation of the Laws, Liberties.
