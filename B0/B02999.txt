ACT Anent the Vintners and Baxters.
These are ordaining all Vintners within this City, or Suburbs, to sell the
Pint of their Mum-Beer for ten shillings Scots the Pint, after Saturday next,
being this ninth of May instant; And whosoever shall contraveen or take any
more, shall be liable in an Fine of twenty Rex-dollars toties quoties. As also,
that all Bread sold by Baxters, and Vintners, be conform to the Standard, both
as to the Weight and Fineness; under the like Penalty.

Extracted by me Jo. RICHARDSON.

Edinburgh, Printed by the Heir of Andrew Anderson, Printer to His most Sacred
Majesty, City and College, Anno Dom. 1685.
