At the Court at WHITEHALL The Tenth of October, 1692.
PRESENT, The Queens most Excellent Majesty in Council.
IT was this Day Ordered by Her Majesty in Council, That the Monthly Fast
appointed by Their Majesty's Proclamation of the 24th Day of March, 1691. Be
for the present Discontinued till further Order; And that his Grace the Lord
Archbishop of Canterbury do cause this Her Majesty's Pleasure to be signified
forthwith to the Ministers of the several Parishes within the Weekly Bills of
Mortality.

JOHN NICHOLAS.

London, Printed by Charles Bill, and the Executrix of Thomas Newcomb deceased;
Printers to the King and Queens most Excellent Majesty's. 1692.
