THE CASE OF George Booth, Esquire To be Considered before the Committee of
Elections, the 22th. of November 1689.
CHarles Boon Esquire Late one of the Burgesses for the Borough of Dartmouth, in
the County of Devon dying; a Writ issued for the Electing of a Burgess for the
said Borough, in the Room of the said Mr. Boon; And the Precept coming to the
proper Officer, the Freemen of the said Borough were Summoned to the Election;
And Mr. Booth had the Majority, by many of the Freemen, who had a Right to
Vote; And was lawfully Returned by the proper Officer.

All which Mr. Booth will make out, at the Hearing before the Committee.
