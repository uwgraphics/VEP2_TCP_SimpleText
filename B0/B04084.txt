November the 22th, 1695.
Subscribers to the land Bank 1695
ACcording to the Settlement of the Land-Bank, Enrolled in Chancery, Anno Dom.
1695; and pursuant to the Notice in the Monthly Account: The present Trustees
and Managers, have appointed Monday the 25th Instant, to choose Twenty One
Trustees and Managers, to continue for one Year.

The Subscribers are desired to bring Twenty One Names in Writing, between the
hours of 9 and 12 in the Forenoon, to Ballat for the Election.

Printed for Thomas Cockerill at the Three Legs in the Poultry; and Edward
Jones in the Savoy, 1695.
