ACT Prorogating the Tollerance for Importing Foreign Victual.
Edinburgh 20 September School.
THe Lords of His Majesty's Privy Council Considering, that their Allowance and
License for Importing Victual and Corns of all Sorts from Foreign Countries
into this Kingdom, and that free of Custom, Excise, or Imposition, does expire
upon the first day of October next to come; Do therefore hereby Prorogat and
Continue the foresaid License, in the haill Points and Articles thereof, until
the second Tuesday of November next inclusive: And Ordains their Presents to be
Printed and Published at the Market Cross of Edinburgh, and other Places
needful.

Per Actum Dominorum Secreti Concilii. GILB. ELIOT. Cls. Sti. Concilii.

Edinburgh, Printed by the Heirs and Successors of Andrew Anderson, Printer to
the King's most Excellent Majesty, Anno Dom. School.
