Mrs. Mehetabel Holt A Person of EARLY PIETY, And quick Understanding in the
Fear of the Lord, Was Born at Newbury in New England, and Died at Bishop-Stoke,
September 30 th. 1677. Aetat. 38.

AMerica Afforded me my Birth,
And Friendly Europe grants me whit'ning Earth;
This Method Christ my Husband did Propone,
To Fashion my vile Body like His Own:
Quietly rest then let my hopeful Dust
Until the Resurrection of the Just,
