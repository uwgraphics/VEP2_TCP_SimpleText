Parents provoke not your Children to wrath, but bring them up in nurture and

A New Catechism for ever. OR, The Mite of a Minister.
Question. WHat is God? and how must he be worshipped?

Answer. John 4.24
God is a Spirit, and they that worship him must worship him in spirit, and in
truth.

Quest. What is the Church of God?

Answ. Ephesians. 1.10
The whole company of Gods Elect, in all places and ages knit by true Faith
unto Christ their head.

Quest. What do you believe concerning God and his Church?

Answ. I believe in God the Father Almighty, maker of Heaven and Earth, and in
Jesus Christ his only Son our Lord, which was conceived by the Holy Ghost,
borne of the Virgin Mary, etc.

I believe in the Holy Ghost, the Holy Catholic Church, etc.

Quest. What is Faith?

Answ. To. 1.17.
An apprehension and applicacation of Christ and all his Merits, as they are
promised and offered in the word and Sacraments.

Quest. What is a Sacrament?

Answ. Romans 4.11.
A Seal of the Covenant of Grace.

Quest. What is the Covenant of Grace?

Answ. Joh. 3.16.
An agreement concerning men to be freely saved through faith in Christ Jesus.

Quest. What is Baptism?

Answ. Mat. 18.19.
A Sacrament of our Ingrafting into Christ, and Entrance into the Church of God.

Quest. What is the Lords Supper

Answ. It is a Sacrament of the eternal Covenant, Ioh. 6.54.
whereby the souls of them that are ingrafted into Christ, are spiritually
nourished unto eternal life.

Who is the Author of this Sacrament? Mat. 26.26.


Answ. The Lord Jesus Christ.

Quest. When did he institute it?

Answ. 1 Cor. 11.23.
In the same night he was betrayed.

Quest. Which is the sign that may be seen?

Answ. Mat. 26.26.27.
Bread and Wine.

Quest. Which is the Grace that cannot be seen?

Answ. 1 Cor. 10, 16.
The Body and Blood of Christ.

Quest. Who are to receive the Lords Supper?

Answ. Such as know their misery by sin, Mat. 11.28.
and the remedy thereof in Christ.

Quest. What must a man do before he can become a worthy receiver?

Answ. 1 Cor. 11, 28.
He must examine himself.

Quest. Why must a man examine himself?

Answ. 1 Cor. 11.27.29.
Because an unworthy Receiver is guilty not only of Christ's Body and Blood;
but he eats and drinks Damnation to himself.

Quest. Wherein must your examination consist?

Answ. 2 Cor. 13.5.
In the Graces both of Faith and Knowledge.

Quest. What other duties are required of you?

Answ. Repentance and Reconciliation. Mat. 5.24.


Quest. What is Repentance?

Answ. Jonah 3.8.
A true turning unto God from every evil way.

Quest. What must you do after you have received?

Answ. Prove. 4.18.
I must endeavor to find increase of Faith, and all other saving Graces;
abounding more and more in well-doing.

Quest. How many Commandments are there?

Answ. Ten.

Quest. Which is the first Commandment?

Answ. Exodus 20.3.
Thou shall have none other Gods but me.

Quest. Which is the second Commandment?

Answ. Exodus 20.4.3.6.
Thou shall not make to thy self any graven Image, nor the likeness of any
thing that is in Heaven above, or in the Earth beneath, or in the waters under
the Earth, thou shall not bow down to them, nor worship them, for I the Lord
thy God am a jealous God, visiting the sias of the Father upon the Children
unto the third and fourth generation, of them that hate me; and show mercy unto
thousands, to them that love me and keep my Commandments.

Quest. The third Commandment.

Answ. Exodus 20.7.
Thou shall not take the Name of the Lord thy God in vain, for the Lord will
not hold him guiltless that takes his Name in vain.

Quest. The fourth Commandment.

Answ. Exodus 20. v. 9.10.11.
Remember thou keep'st holy the Sabbath Day, six days shall thou labor and
do all that thou have to do; but the seventh day is the Sabbath of the Lord
thy God: in it thou shall do no manner of work, thou nor thy Son, nor thy
Daughter, thy Manservant, nor thy Maidservant, thy Cattle, nor thy stranger
that is within thy gates; for in six days, the Lord made Heaven and Earth,
and all that in them is, and rests the seventh day; therefore the Lord
blessed the seventh day, and hallowed it.

Quest. The fifth Commandment.

Answ. Exodus 20, 11.
Honor thy Father and thy Mother, that thy days may be long in the land which
the Lord thy God gives thee.

Quest. The sixth Commandment.

Answ. Thou shall not kill.

Quest. The seventh Commandment.

Answ. Thou shall not commit Adultery

Quest. The eighth Commandment.

Answ. Thou shall not steal.

Quest. The ninth Commandment.

Answ. Thou shall not bear false witness against thy Neighbor.

Quest. The tenth Commandment.

Answ. Thou shall not covet thy neighbors house, thou shall not covet thy
neighbors Wife, nor his Manservant, nor his Maidservant, nor his Ox, nor
his Ass nor any thing that is his.

Quest. What is Prayer?

Answ. 1 John 5.14.
A calling upon God in the Name of Christ Jesus, according to his will.

Quest: After what manner did our Savior teach his Disciples to pray?

Answ. After this manner.

Our Father which art in Heaven, Mat. 6.9.10.11.12.
hallowed be thy Name, thy Kingdom come, thy will be done in Earth, as it is in
Heaven, give us this day our daily Bread, and forgive us our trespasses as we
forgive them that trespass against us, and lead us not into temptation, but
deliver us from evil, for thine is the Kingdom, the power, and thy glory, for
ever and ever, Amen.
