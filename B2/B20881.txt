AT Mr. Croomes, at the sign of the Shoo and Slap near the Hospital-gate in
West-Smithfield, is to be seen The Wonder of Nature viz. A Girl above Fifteen
years of Age, born ^n Cheshire, and not much above Eighteen Inches long, having
never a perfect Bone in any part of her, only the Head, yet she has all her
Senses to admiration; and Discourses, Reads very well, Sings* Whistles, and all
very pleasant to hear!
