ALL that wish well to the safety of this Kingdom, in this City of London,
and parts adjacent, which did not appear on Tuesday last, are desired to meet
at Grocers Hall to morrow, being Thursday the 20. of this instant July, between
the hours of eight in the morning, and eight at night (to which place a
Commit of the House of Commons do purposely adjourne) to receive such
Propositions as concern Sir William Waller, the present state of the Western
parts, and welfare of the whole Kingdom.

Show this to your Friends.

If it be stuck up, let none presume to pull it down.
