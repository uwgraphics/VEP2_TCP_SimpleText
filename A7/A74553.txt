blazon of the Commonwealth
AN ORDINANCE Appointing the EXCISE OF Alum and Copperace.
BE it Ordained by His Highness the Lord Protector, with the advice and
consent of His Council, That the Commissioners for the Excise and their
sub-Commissioners and all Farmers and Officers employed in the service of the
Excise; Do from and after the nine and twentieth day of September, One thousand
six hundred fifty four, demand and receiv for the duty of Excise, three pence
for and upon every hundred weight, containing five score and twelv pounds of
Alum, and no more; and the same rate for Copperace to be paid by the maker
thereof, the rate in the Ordinance of the seventeenth of March, One thousand
six hundred fifty three, notwithstanding.

Tuesday, 29 August, 1654.
ORdered by His Highness the Lord PROTECTOR and the Council, That this
Ordinance be forthwith Printed and Published.

Hen. Scobel, Clerk of the Council.
