By the Master and Wardens of the Company of Stationers. To all Freemen of the
said Company.
YOu are required (according to your Oath) to make your appearance at
Stationers Hall on Monday the thirtieth day of this present June, at eight of
the Clock in the morning, being Quarter day, and a Common Hall; whereof you are
not to fail, and you are desired not to depart until the Court shall dismiss
you.

Stationers Hall the 21th of June, 1645.
