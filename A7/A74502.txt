AN ORDINANCE For Continuation of an Act, Entitled, An Act for laying an
Imposition upon Coals, toward the Building and Maintaining Ships for Guarding
the Seas.
Be it Ordained by his Highness the Lord PROTECTOR, with the advice and
consent of his Council, That one Act of Parliament, entitled, An Act for laying
an Imposition upon Coals toward the Building and maintaining Ships for
Guarding the Seas; And all Powers and Clauses therein contained be, and are
hereby continued, and shall and do stand in full force, until the 26 day of
March, 1655.
