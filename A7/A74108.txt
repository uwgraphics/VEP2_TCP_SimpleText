Die Sabbathi, 8. Julii, 1648.
ORdered by the Commons assembled in Parliament, That Thanks be given to
Almighty God, to morrow, the Ninth of this instant July, in all the Churches
and Chapels within the late Lines of Communication, for the great Victory it
pleased God to give the Parliament Forces under the Command of Colonel Edward
Rossiter, over the Enemies Forces in Willoughby-fields in the County of
Nottingham: And that the Lord Major be desired to give notice hereof to the
several Ministers of those several Places.

H. Elsynge, Cler. Parl. D. Com.
