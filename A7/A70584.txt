The Conditions of Sale.
I. THE highest Bidder is the Buyer, etc. II. The Books, for ought we know, are
Perfect; if any appear otherwise, before they be taken away, the Buyer has his
Choice of taking or leaving them. III. That every Person give in his Name and
Place of Abode; paying also Five Shillings in the Pound for what he shall Buy,
and be obliged to take them away, and pay the remaining Part of the Money at
the Place of Sale, within Three Days after it be ended; the Buyer also paying
Porteridge. IV. None to bid less than Sixpence, when a Bundle rises to Ten
Shillings. V. That any that Buys by Commission, being distant from the Place of
Sale, do pay the Carriage home.
