AN ORDINANCE FOR Continuing an Act FOR Impressing of Seamen.
Be it Ordained by his Highness, the Lord PROTECTOR, by and with the advice
and consent of his Council, That one Act of Parliament, entitled, An Act for
Impressing of Seamen; And all Powers and Clauses therein contained, be, and
are hereby revived, and shall continue, and stand in full force, until the
first day of Novem^^^ (...) the year one thousand six hundred fifty (...).

Thursday the 23. of March. 1653.
ORdered by His Highness the Lord PROTECTOR and the Council, That this
Ordinance be forthwith Printed and Published.

HEN. SCOBEL, Clerk of the Council.
