A true Copy of His Majesty's Message sent to the Houses of Parliament by the
Earl of Dunfermeline.
Saturday June 5th 1647
I am commanded by His Majesty to make known to the Houses of Parliament.

1. THat His Majesty went from Holdenby unwillingly.

2. That Hedesires they will neglect no means for preservation of the Honor of
the Parliament, and the established Laws of the Land.

3 That they will believe nothing that is said or done in His Name against the
Parliament, until they send unto Himself, and know the truth of it.

London printed June the 8th 1647
