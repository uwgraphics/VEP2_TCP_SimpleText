A Catalogue of Valuable Books, all in Folio, with some Curious and Fair
Manuscripts, will be sold by Auction on Monday the 12th of March next, between
2 and 3 in the Afternoon, at the Golden Anchor, an Upholsterers, amongst the
Woollen-Drapers in St. Paul's Churchyard; where Catalogues are distributed
Gratis; at Mr. Notts in the Pellmell, at Mr. Welds at the Crown, between the
two Temple-gates in Fleetstreet, and at Mr. Walsals, at the Heart and Bible, on
the VVest-end of the Royal Exchange, Cornhil.
Conditions of Sale.
I. That he that bids most is the Buyer, but if any difference arise which the
Company cannot decide, then the Book or Books shall be exposed to Sale again.

II. That all the Books in this Catalogue (for ought we know) are perfect, but
if any appear to be otherwise, before they are taken away, the Buyer shall be
at liberty to take or leave them.

III. That the Money for the Books so bought, is to be paid within three days
after the Auction is ended. The Books will be exposed to view two days before
the Sale.
