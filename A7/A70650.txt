Chacers Embroiderers andC. Where you may find a CIPHER for any NAME whatsoever
Curiously Composed after the newest Mode. BY Jeremiah Marlow N: Glascock Sculp.

London printed for Mr: Rogers at the Sun against St. Dunstans Church in Fleet
Street 1683
