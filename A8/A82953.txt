DIEV ET MON DROIT

HONI SOIT QVI MAL Y PENSE

royal blazon or coat of arms
Die Mercurii 9. Maii, 1660.
ORdered by the Lords and Commons in Parliament Assembled, That the Arms of
this Commonwealth where ever they are standing, be forthwith taken down, and
that the Kings Majesty's Arms be set up in stead thereof.

Die Mercurii 9. Maii* 1660.
ORdered by the Lords in Parliament Assembled, That this Order be forthwith
Printed and Published.

JO. BROWN, Cleric. Parliamentorum.

London, Printed by John Macock, and Francis Tyton, Printers to the House of
Lords, 1660.
