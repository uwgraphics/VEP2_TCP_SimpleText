BE it Enacted by this present Parliament, and by the Authority thereof, That
this present Easter Term 1659. be continued to all intents and purposes, as if
Judges Authorized by Act of Parliament sat in Court.

ORdered by the Parliament, That this Act be forthwith Printed and Published.

Passed the 14th of May 1659. JO. PHELPES Clerk of the Parliament pro tempore.

LONDON, Printed by JOHN FIELD, Printer to the Parliament.

And are to be sold at the seven Stars in Fleetstreet, over against Dunstans
Church, 1659.
