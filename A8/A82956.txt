Die Mercurij 16. April, 1645.
ORdered by the Lords and Commons in Parliament Assembled, That the Lord Major
of the City of London is hereby desired and required to give direction that
Public Thanksgiving be made on the next Lords day, in every Church, and
Chapel within the Lines of Communication, and Bills of Mortallity for Gods
blessing to the Forces in Scotland, against the Rebels in that Kingdom.

Jo: Brown Cler. Parliamentorum.
To the Gentleman Usher attending this House, or his Deputy to be delivered to
the Lord Major of the City of London.
