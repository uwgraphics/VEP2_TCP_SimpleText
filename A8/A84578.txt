AN ACT Establishing the POWERS OF Lord Admiral of England, and Lord Warden
of the Cinque Ports, UPON THE COUNCIL of STATE.
BE it Enacted by this present Parliament, and by the Authority thereof, That
the Council of State appointed by Authority of Parliament for the year ensuing,
shall have and exercise all such Power, Jurisdiction and Authority, and are
hereby authorized and enabled to do and execute all such things as pertain to
the Office of Lord Admiral of England, and Lord Warden of the Cinque Ports, and
all such other Powers and Authorities as were vested in the Council of State
for the year past, by force of an Act of Parliament, Entitled, An Act
Repealing the Powers formerly given to the Lord Admiral, and transferring it to
the Council of State. Provided, That this Act do continue and stand in force
until the First day of July, One thousand six hundred fifty and one.

Die Jovis, 13 Februarii, 1650.
ORdered by the Parliament, That this Act be forthwith printed and published.

Hen: Scobell, Cleric. Parliamenti.

London, Printed by John Field, Printer to the Parliament of England, 1650.
