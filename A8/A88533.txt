A LOOKING GLASS FOR THE SOVLE, Worthy to be hung up in every House in this
Kingdom, and to be looked in daily; for herein is to be seen what sins tend
to the breach of every one of Gods Commandments, whereby every one of us may
clearly see, how far forth we have had a hand in pulling down this heavy
Judgment of War upon this Nation. And so consequently, what cause we have
to humble our selves, and pray, and seek Gods face, and turn from our evil
ways.
