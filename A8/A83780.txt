Die Jovis, 8. Julii, 1646.
ORdered by the Commons Assembled in Parliament, That all such Persons as have
presented their Petitions at Goldsmiths-Hall, or agreed to their Compositions,
and shall not come in before the the first of August next, and prosecute their
Compositions to effect, shall lose the whole benefit of the favor intended by
their Compositions, and be reputed among those that still stand out, and have
not rendered themselves to the Parliament.

This to be forthwith Printed and published: And that the care hereof be
referred to the Committee at Goldsmiths-Hall.

H. Elsynge Cler. Parl. D. Com.
