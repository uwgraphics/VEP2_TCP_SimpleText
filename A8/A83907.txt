An ORDER of the Lords Spiritual and Temporal assembled at Westminster, For the
not Observing the sixth day of FEBRUARY.
Dies SABBATI Febr. 2d. 1788/9.
ORdered by the Lords Spiritual and Temporal assembled at Westminster, That the
day of Thanksgiving on the sixth of February instant, on which day the late K:
James 2d: came to the Throne, shall not be Observed in this Kingdom.

Ja: Broun Clericus Parliamentoram.

London, Printed by Edward Jones, for James Patridge and Samnel Heyrick. 1688/9.
