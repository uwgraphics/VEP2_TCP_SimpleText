Die Lunae 5 Iunii. 1648.
REsolved upon the question, by the Lords and Commons in Parliament assembled,
That the fifth and twentieth part be Leavied upon none, but such Delinquents as
are within the Ordinances of Sequestrations.

ORdered by the Lords assembled in Parliament, that this Order be forthwith
printed and published.

Joh. Brown, Cler. Parliamentorum.

Imprinted at London for John Wright, at the Kings Head in the Old-Bayley. 1648.
