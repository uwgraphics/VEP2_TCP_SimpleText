Die Lunae10. Martii, 1644.
IT is this day Ordered by the Commons assembled in Parliament; That the day of
public Thanksgiving, appointed by both Houses to be kept on Wednesday next
the 12th. of this instant March, be observed on the same day, in all the
Churches and Chapels within the Lines of Communication. And the Assembly of
Divines are desired to take notice hereof, and to employ their best endeavors
and care herein. And my Lord Major is further desired to give notice hereof to
all the several and respective Ministers.

H. Elsynge Cler. Parl. D. Com.
