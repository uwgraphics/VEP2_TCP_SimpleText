Die Sabbathi 8 Julii 1648.
ORdered by the Lords and Commons assembled in Parliament, That on the next
Lords day public thanks be given to Almighty God, in all Churches within the
City of London, and late Lines of Communication, for his great mercy in giving
the Parliaments Forces a great Victory in the North. And the Lord Mayor is
desired to give notice hereof, to the Ministers in the several Parishes within
the City.

Joh Brown Cler. Parliamentorum.

London printed for John Wright at the Kings Head in the Old Bayley. 1648.
