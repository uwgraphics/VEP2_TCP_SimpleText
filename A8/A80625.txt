MILK FOR BABES.

DRAWN Out of the Breasts of both TESTAMENTS.

Chiefly, for the spiritual nourishment of Boston Babes in either England: But
may be of like use for any Children.

By JOHN COTTON, B. D. and Teacher to the Church of Boston in New England.

LONDON, Printed by J. Coe, for Henry Overton, and are to be sold at his Shop,
in Pope's Head Alley. 1646. April 9th.
