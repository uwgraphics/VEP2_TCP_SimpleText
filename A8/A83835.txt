Die Mercurii, 25 Julii, 1649.
REsolved upon the question by the Commons assembled in Parliament, That the
Tenants of Sequestered Estates who are to retain their Rents in their hands till
the First of August next, shall continue their Rents for such Estate in their
hands till the First of September, except the Parliament shall otherwise Order.

Resolved, etc.

Hen: Scobell, Cleric. Parliamenti.

London, Printed for Edward Husband, Printer to the Parliament of England. July
26. 1649.
