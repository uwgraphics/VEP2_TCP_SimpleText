Die Lunae, 6. Martii, 1647.
ORdered by the Lords and Commons in Parliament assembled, That Power be, and
is hereby given to the Commissioners at Goldsmiths-Hall to send for all such
Delinquents in safe Custody, that either do refuse to Compound at all, or
having submitted to a Composition, do not with effect prosecute and perfect
their Composition, and take out their Ordinances passed both Houses within the
times limited, or within one Month next after the date of this Order; and to
commit to Prison such as they shall find cause, till they do comply and yield
obedience accordingly.

H. Elsynge, Cler. Parl. D. Com.
