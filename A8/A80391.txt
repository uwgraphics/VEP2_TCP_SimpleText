THe Contractors for sale of the Lands and Possessions of the late King, Queen
and Prince, have resolved to begin their sittings for Sales upon Monday the
Fourth of March, 1649. as to all such of the said Lands (only) before that
time Surveyed, and Certified to the Register, whereof there shall be immediate
Tenancies; from which day the respective preemptions of the immediate Tenants
are to begin: And for all such of the Lands, whereof there are such immediate
Tenancies, and whereof the Surveys shall be returned after that day, the said
respective preemptions to commence according to a late Additional Act of the
18th of Febr. 1649.

William Tayleure Clerk, attending the Contractors.
