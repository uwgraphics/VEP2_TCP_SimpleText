Die Veneris, 6o Septembr. 1650.
ORdered by the Parliament, That the Lords Commissioners of the Great-Seal of
England do consider how the Engagement may be tendered to all persons in any
Cities, Towns or Counties within England or Wales, who are willing to take the
Engagement: And that the said Lords Commissioners be Authorized and Required to
nominate and appoint fit persons in the several Counties, Cities, Towns or
places to take such Subscriptions, in such sort as they had formerly power to
do by the Act, the time thereby limited being elapsed, and to cause Returns to
be made thereof according to the said Act, by such times as they shall appoint.

Hen: Scobell, Cleric. Parliamenti.

London, Printed by Edward Husband and John Field, Printers to the Parliament
of England, 1650.
