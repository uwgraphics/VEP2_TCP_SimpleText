Spiritual MILK FOR BOSTON BABES in either England.

Drawn out of the Breasts of both TESTAMENTS, for their souls nourishment: But
may be of like use for any Children.

By JOHN COTTON B. D. and Teacher to the Church of Boston in New England.

LONDON, Printed for Henry Cripps, in Popes-head-Alley. 1657.

MILK FOR BABES.
Q. WHat has GOD done for you?

A. God has made me, Psalm. 119.73. Psalm. 120.4, 5, 6, 7, 8. Isa 63.1.
He keeps me, and he can save me.

Q. Who is God?

A. God is a spirit of Jo. 4.24. Exo. 3.14.
himself, and for himself.

Q. How many Gods be there?

A. There is but one God in three Persons, 1 Cor. 8.6. 1 John 5.7.
the Father, the Son, and the Holy Ghost.

Q. How did God make you?

A. In my first parents Genesis 1.26, 27. Eccles. 7.29.
holy and righteous.

Q. Are you then born holy and righteous?

A. No, my first Father Isa. 43.27. To. 11.10.
sinned and I in him.

Q. Are you then born a sinner?

A. I was conceived in sin, and Ps. 51 5.
born in iniquity.

Q. What is your birth-sin?

Answ. Adams sin imputed to me Romans 5.12, 13, 14. Ep. 14.12.
and a corrupt nature dwelling in me.

Q What is your corrupt nature?

Answ. My corrupt nature is empty of Rom 7.18. Genesis 6.5.
Grace, bent unto sin, and only unto sin, and that continually.

Qu. What is sin?

A. Sin is the 1 John 3.4.
transgression of the Law.

Q. How many Commandments of the Law be there?

A. Deuteronomy. 4.13.
Ten.

Q. What is the first Commandment?

A. Thou shall have no other Gods but Exo. 20.3.
me.

Q. What is the meaning of this Commandment?

Answ. That we should Math. 4.10.
worship the only true God, and no other beside him.

Q. What is the second Commandment?

A. Thou shall not make to thy self any graven image, Exo. 20.4, 5.
etc.

Q. What is the meaning of this Commandment?

Answ. That we should worship the true Job 4.24 Deu^ 12.32 Mat. 15 9
God with true worship; such as God has ordained, not such as man has
invented.

Q. What is the third Commandment?

A. Thou shall not take the name of the Lord thy God in vain, etc.

Q. What is here meant by the Name of God?

A. God Deuteronomy. 28.58. Ex. 33.19. with 34.6. 1 Kin 9.3. Psa. 1.8 ^. Job 1 21.
himself and the good things of God, whereby he is known as a man by his Name,
as his Attributes, worship, word, and works.

Q. What is it not to take his Name in vain?

Ans. To make use of god, and the good things of God, to his glory, and our 2

Lev. 16 2. Heb. 4.2.

Qu. What is the fourth Commandment?

A. Remember that thou keep holy the Exodus 20.8, 9, 10, 11.
Sabbath day, etc.

Q. What is the meaning of this Commandment?

A. That we should rest from labor and much more from play on Isa. 58.12, 13.
Exodus 32.6.
the Lords day, that we may draw nigh to God in holy duties.

Quest. What is the fifth Commandment?

Answ. Honor thy Father and thy Mother, Exo. 20.12.
that thy days may be long in the land, which the Lord thy God gives thee.

Quest. Who are here meant by father and mother?

Answ. All our Superiors, whether in Pro. 23.22. 1 Kings 5.13. and 2.12. 1

Quest. What is the honor due to them?

Answ. Reverence, Mal. 16. Heb 12.9. Ephesians. 6.1. 1 Timothy 5.4.

Quest. What is the sixth Commandment?

Answ. Thou shall do no Exo. ^0.13.

Answ. That we should not shorten the Mat. 5.22. 2 Cor. 7.10. 1 Samuel 26.24.
Mark. 3 4.

Q. What is the seventh Commandment?

Answ. Thou shall not commit Exo. 20 14.
Adultery.

Quest. What is the sin here forbidden?

Answ. To defile our selves, or others Genesis 9. Lev. 10.24.

Quest. What is the duty here commanded?

Answ. Chastity, to possess Tit. 2.5. 1 Pet. 3.2. 1 These. 4.4, 5.

Quest. What is the eighth Commandment?

Answ. Thou shall not Exo. 20.15.

Quest. What is the stealth here forbidden?

Answ. To take away Luke 19.8. 1 These. 4.6. Prove. 21.20.

Quest. What is the duty here commanded?

Answ. To get our goods Prove. 13.11. John 6.12. Luke 19.16. and 15.13.

Quest. What is the ninth Commandment?

A. Thou Exo. 20.16.

Quest. What is the sin here forbidden?

Answ. To Ep. 4.25. Jer. 9.4, 5. Josh 9.22. Prove. 13.7.

Quest. What is the duty here required?

A. Truth Ephesians. 4 25 Mat. 23.23. Ex. 20.17. Rom 7.7. Heb. 13.5.
and faithfulness.

Quest. What is the tenth Commandment?

A. Thou shall not covet, etc.

Quest. What is the coveting here forbidden?

Answ. Lust after the things of other men; and want of contentment with our own.

Qu. Whether have you kept all these Commandments?

Answ. To* 7.14. and 3.23.
No, I and all men are sinners.

Quest. What is the wages of sin?

Answ. Jo 6.23. and 2.8, 9.
Death and damnation.

Quest. How look you then to be saved?

Answ. Acts 4.12.
Only by Jesus Christ.

Quest. Who is Jesus Christ?

A. The eternal Son of God, who for our sakes 1 John 3.22. Pro. 8.23, 24, 25.
Joh. 1.14. 2 Cor. 8.9. Luke 1.68, 69. and 2.11.
became man, that he might redeem and save us.

Q. How does Christ redeem and save us?

A. By his righteous life, and bitter death, and glorious resurrection to life
Gal. 4.4, 5. Mat. 20.28. Phil. 2.7, 8. Romans 4.25.
again.

Q. How do we come to have part and fellowship with Christ in his death and
Resurrection?

A. By the power of his John 17.20, 21. 2 Cor. 5.20. 1 Cor. 6.17

Q. What is his Word?

A. The 2 Timothy 3.15, 16.
Holy Scriptures of the Prophets and Apostles, the Old and New Testament, Law
and Gospel.

Q. How does the Ministry of the Law bring you toward Christ?

A. By bringing me to know my sin, and the wrath of God against me for it. Romans
3 20. Gal. 3.10.24. To. 7, 9, 10.


Q. What are you thereby the nearer to Christ?

A. So I come to feel my cursed estate, and need of a Savior.

Quest. How does the Ministry of the Gospel help you in this cursed Estate?

Answ. By humbling me Luke 3.4, 5, 6.
yet more, and then raising me up out of this Estate.

Quest. How does the Ministry of the Gospel humble you more?

Answ. By revealing the 2 Timothy 1.10. 1 Timothy 1.15. John 16.7.9. and 6.44. Lu. 19
10.

Quest. How then does the Ministry of the Gospel raise you up out of this
lost estate to come unto Christ.

Answ. By John 12.31. 2 Cor. 5.19, 20. Cant. 5.10. to 16. with 6.1. 2 Cor.
3.28. Lu. 19.10. Mat. 9.13. Gal. 3.5. Romans 10.17. with ^.61.

Answ. Faith is a grace of Phil. 1.29. 2 Cor. 4.13. Phil. 3.8, 9.

Quest. What is Prayer?

Answ. It is calling upon Mat. 6.9. John 16.23. Romans 8.26, 27. John 5 14.

Answ. Newness of life is a Psalm. 51.1, 2.10.

A. It is 2 Cor. 8.5. and 9.13. Genesis 17.7.
the profession of that Covenant, which God has made with his faithful
people, to be a God unto them and to their seed.

A. By confession of Mat. 3.6. 2 Cor. 9.13. Acts 2.38, 39.

and also of my Resurrection from the death at the last day.

together with Christ on his Throne of Glory at the last judgment.
