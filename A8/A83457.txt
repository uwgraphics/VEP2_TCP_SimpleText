Thursday, January 5. 1659. Resolved,
THat upon the Whole Matter of the Report touching Absent Members, The
Parliament does Adjudge and Declare, That the Members who stand Discharged from
Voting or Sitting as Members of this House, in the years 1648. 1649. do stand
duly Discharged by Judgment of Parliament, from Sitting as Members of this
Parliament* during this Parliament. And it is Ordered, That Writs do Issue
forth for Electing New Members in Their Places.

Ordered by the Parliament,
THat this Vote be forthwith Printed and Published.

THOMAS St NICHOLAS, Clerk of the Parliament.

LONDON, Printed by John Streater, and John Macock, Printers to the Parliament,
1659.
