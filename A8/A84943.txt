THE FRVITS OF FAITH IN THESE FIVE FAMOUS Men, SCRIPTURE WORTHIES HEB: XI.
Enoch.
By faith Enoch was translated that he should not see death: etc: for before
his translation he had this testimony that he pleased God. verse. 5.

portrait

Noah.
By faith Noah being warned of God of things not seen as yet, moved with fear
prepared an ark to ye saving of his house etc. v. 7.

portrait

Abraham.
By faith Abraham when he was called to go out etc. obeyed and went out not
knowing whither he went and he sojourned in the land of promise etc. v. 8. 9.

portrait

Joseph.
By faith Joseph when he died (in Egypt) made mention of the departing of the
children of Israel, and gave commandment concerning his bones. v. 12.

portrait

Moses.
By faith Moses when he was come to years refused to he called the son of
Pharaohs daughter, choosing rather to suffer affliction etc. v. 24. 25.

THE PICTURE OF A NEWBORN CHRISTIAN DRAWN BY THE SCRIPTUR'S TRUE PROPORTION,
IN TWENTY FOUR INFALLIBLE CHARACTERS.
1. Of his new Birth. Except a man be Borne again he cannot enjoy God being
Borne again of incorruptable seed, 1 pet. 1. 23. Whosoever is borne of God he
cannot sin, 1. John 3. 9. cast not the childrens bread to dogs, Math. 15. 26.

2. of his new Food. I have esteemed his word more then my necessary food Job.
23. 12 How sweet are thy words unto my taste? psalm. 119. 103 As christ's, so in
the measure received the christian his meatis to do the will of his father Joh.
4. 34.

3. of his Growth. may grow up into him in all things Ephesians. 4. 15. As newborn
babes desire the sincere milk of the word to grow thereby, 1. pet. 1. 2. But
they grow in grace and in knowledge, 1. pet. 1. 2.

4. his Apparel Has put on the armor of light Rom: 13. 12 And of
righteousness. 2. Cor. 6. 7. the whole armor of God Ephe: 6: 11.

5. his inward man Thy word have I hid in my heart psalm. 119. 11: Your life is
hid with Christ in God Colo: 3. 3. but let it be the hidden man of the heart
strengthened with might by his spirit. etc. Ep: 3. 16

6. his will. for to will is present with me To: 7. 18. with good will doing
service as to ye Lord for if there be first a willing mind it is accepted.
etc. 2. Cor: 8. 12.

7. his Conscience always a conscience void of offense toward God and toward
men Act. 24: 16. Holding faith and a good conscience. 1 Timothy 1. 19 with the
testimony of our conscience serving god 2. Co 1. 12

8. his Love. o how I love thy law, yea above fine gold. Psalm: 119. love
works none ill, love is the fulfilling of the law. Rom: 13. 10.

9. his Hatred. I hate every false way psalm. 119. 104. hate the evil love the
good Amos. 5. 15. do good to them that hate you Mat: 5. 44. therefore the world
hates you John. 15. 19

10. his Fear. who desire to fear thy name Nehe: 1. 11. therefore will not
we fear though the earth be removed Psalm. 46. 2. a book of remembrance was
written for them. Mal. 3. 16

11. his Confidence And have no confidence in the flesh Phil: 3. 3. We hold
fast the confidence firm unto the end Heb: 3. 6. cast not away your confidence
which has great recompense Heb. 10. 35.

12. his Joy. Yet I will rejoice in the Lord: I will joy in the God of my
salvation. Habak: 3. 28. and your joy no man takes from you Jo: 16. 22. It is
joy to the just to do judgment Pro: 21: 15.

13. his Grief but they are grieved for the affliction of Joseph And grieve not
the holy spirit of God. Ephesians. 4. 30. Surely he has borne our griefs, Isa. 53. 4

14. his Patience Who by patient continuance in well-doing seek for glory, and
honor, Romans 1. 7. and let us run with patience Heb. 12. 1. let patience have
her perfect work, sa. 1. 4.

15. his Anger. Cease from anger, and forsake wrath: fret not thy self in any
wise to do evil. Psalm. 37. 8. be angry and sin not Ephesians. 4. 26. put of anger
wrath malice etc.

16. his outward man Let not sin reign therefore in your mortal body etc: Romans
6. 12. but if ye through the spirit do mortify the deeds of the body Rom: 8.
13

17. his Hearing. take heed how ye hear Luke 8. 28. take heed what ye hear. my
sheep hear my voice Joh. 10. 27. faith comes by hearing To. 10. 17.

18. his Eyes. I was eyes to the blind Job. 29. 15. the eyes of the lord are
in every place beholding the evil and the good Pro. 15. 3. blessed are the eyes
which see Luke 10. 23.

19. his Tongue. not double tongued. 1. Tim. 3. 8. Who so keeps his tongue
keeps his soul: pr: 21 The tongue of the Just is as choice silver Pro. 10.
20.

20. his Eating. and eats not the bread of idleness. pr: 32. They shall eat
and praise the Lord Isa. 62. 9 Thou shall eat the labor of thine hands Psalm.
128. 2.

21. his Hands. He that, (...) has clean hands and a pure heart, psalm. 24. 4.
lifting up holy hands. 1. Tim. 2. 8. work with your own hands. 1. These. 4. 11.

22. his Feet. Keep thy foot when thou go to the house of God. Eccl. 5. 1.
make strait paths for your feet. Heb. 12. 13 if thy foot offend thee cut it of.
mat. 18. 8.

23. his Company. I am a companion of all them that fear thee. psalm. 119. 63.
not to company with fornicators. 1. Cor. 5. 9 note yt man and have no company.
etc 2 Th. 3. 11

24. his Death. Precious in the sight of the Lord, ps. 116. 15. on such the
second death has no power. Rev. 25 He yt overcomes shall not be hurt of the
second death Rev. 2. 11.

Finis.
