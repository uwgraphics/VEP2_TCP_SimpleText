IIII. Church, and so continue (as I have intimated from our sad experience) an
endless Separating until this first Separation shall in a few years be able to
take up the saying of that Greatest Grandmother, unto those many Schisms she
shall see issuing, as her natural Offspring out of her own Bowels: Rise up
daughter, go to thy Daughter, for thy Daughters Daughter has a Daughter: for
this Seperations Separation, has a Separation.
