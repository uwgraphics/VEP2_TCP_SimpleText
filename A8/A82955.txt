Die Veneris 11. Junii 1647.
ORdered by the Lords and Commons assembled in Parliament, That the Excise of
Flesh be taken off, from and after the four and twentieth day of this instant
June, and that from and after the said day the said Excise cease and be no
longer paid.

Joh. Brown Cler. Parliamentorum.

Die Veneris 11. Junii 1647.
ORdered by the Lords and Commons assembled in Parliament, That the Excise of
Salt made in this Kingdom be taken off, from and after the four and
twentieth day of this instant June, and that from and after the said day, the
said Excise cease and be no longer paid.

Joh. Brown Cler. Parliamentorum.

London printed for John Wright at the Kings Head in the Old Bayley. 1647.
