Monday June 27th 1659. Resolved,
THat this Parliament does Declare, that, for the Encouragement of a Godly,
Preaching, Learned Ministry throughout the Nation, the Payment of Tithes shall
continue as now they are, unless this Parliament shall find out some other
more equal and Comfortable Maintenance, both for the Ministry, and satisfaction
of the People.

Resolved,
THat this Vote be Printed and published.

THO. St NICHOLAS Clerk of the Parliament.

LONDON: Printed by John Field and Henry Hills, Printers to the PARLIAMENT.
