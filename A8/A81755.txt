The Drunkards Character.
A Drunkard is the annoyance of Modesty, The trouble of Civility, The spoil of
Wealth, The distraction of Reason, He is only the Brewers Agent, The Tavern
and Alehouse Benefactor, The Beggars Companion, The Constables Trouble: He is
his wifes VVoe, His childrens Sorrow, His neighbors Scoff, His own Shame. In
sum, He is a tubb of Swill, A spirit of Sleep, A picture of a Beast, And a
monster of Men.
