DIEV ET MON DROIT

HONI SOIT QVI MAL Y PENSE

royal blazon or coat of arms
Thursday November 15. 1660.
THat the Privilege of this House, in Point of Protection from Arrests, does
belong to the Members of this House, and their Menial servants only, and to
the Officers attending the service of this House; And that all Protections in
Writeing, granted by any Member of this House, to any Person whatsoever, be
forthwith called in, and are hereby Declared to be Null and Void. And all
Persons whom this may concern, are required, upon all occasions, to take notice
of the Privilege belonging to the Members of this House, and their Menial
servants, and to the Officers attending the service of this House, at their
Peril.

REsolved, That this Order be forthwith Printed and Published.

W. JESSOP, Clerk of the Commons House of Parliament.

LONDON* Printed by John Bill, Printer to the King's most Excellent MAJESTY.
1660.
