Saturday, January 7* 1659.
Ordered by the Parliament,
THat all Mayors* Justices of the peace, and all other Officers and Ministers,
whom it does or may Concern* be, and are hereby Required and Enjoined to see,
That all Acts and Laws, for the Due and Strict Observation of the Lords Day be
put in effectual Execution.

Ordered,
THat this Vote be forthwith Printed and Published.

THOMAS St NICHOLAS, Clerk of the Parliament.

LONDON, Printed by John Streater, and John Macock, Printers to the Parliament,
1659.
